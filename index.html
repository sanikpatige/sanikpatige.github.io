About Me

Building Data Infrastructure at Scale

I'm a Data Engineer and MLOps Engineer specializing in building robust data pipelines, orchestrating machine learning workflows, and deploying production-grade ML systems.

Beyond handling data, I'm driven by the challenge of turning complex raw inputs into reliable, usable systems. I enjoy designing pipelines that power insights and apply engineering principles to build for scale, speed, and stability.
